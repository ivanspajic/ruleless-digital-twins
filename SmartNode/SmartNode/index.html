Guide

What the assistant understands

Commands are resolved against the live Home Assistant catalog. Generic energy price intents are routed to the dedicated price endpoint.

LightsTurn individual lights on or off, or switch all lights off.
SwitchesControl switches such as purifiers or demo loads.
ClimateSet thermostat or input number temperature targets.
SensorsAsk for state readings from queryable Home Assistant entities.
Energy priceAsk generic price questions to use /api/price.
Scenes & ScriptsTrigger available Home Assistant scenes and scripts by name.
